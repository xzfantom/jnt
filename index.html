About

Jonathan Ginter spent 8 years as a performing artist, followed by 30 years in technology. Starting at a young age, Jonathan was exposed to a broad education at home, including history, literature, anthropology, philosophy, and music. Jonathan’s parents were professors and teachers who provided him with the skills to teach and coach others. He has honed those skills through years of helping others overcome their roadblocks, whether professional or personal. Throughout his life, Jonathan has maintained a constant fascination with quantum physics and its implications on the nature of time and reality. That interest has routinely co-existed with his interest in psychology, world religion, and philosophy. |The unification of those interests have found their ultimate expression in the way he has chosen to live his life and in the pages of this book. Today, Jonathan travels the world, helping others learn his Guided Intuition techniques.
