Sensory immersion & meditation

Using a combination of sensory immersion, meditation, and intuition, Jonathan can help you explore your future goals, bringing them to life. The more vividly your goals are in your mind, the faster you will progress along your path towards them. Conviction drives progress and the best way to create conviction is to have experienced the reality that you want.

Interview process

Using a dynamic interview process, Jonathan will lead you through the sights, sounds, smells, and feelings of the reality you are trying to achieve. That experience can then be expressed in a wide variety of ways, making it easier for you to reproduce it at a moment's notice.

Feeling lost?

This exercise is especially recommended for those who do not know what they want anymore. Your intuition already knows where you are headed. Jonathan can help you tap into that destination, allowing you to explore it in all of its detail.
