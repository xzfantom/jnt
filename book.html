The Intuitive Way

Achieving your goals without stress or fear using Guided Intuition

We live in a reality defined by probability theory. Every moment, our choices move us through an invisible maze of branching realities. We are blindly navigating an infinite set of paths that lead to every possible outcome, including the one we want most. Our intuition is the key to navigating that maze successfully. Our biggest is our own fear.

Intuition is a powerful navigational tool that we seldom use. It can accurately lead us towards any goal that we set. Listening to it is a skill that can be developed. However, intuition requires guidance. It needs to know where we want to go and it will listen to the strongest signal it can find. We create a signal by setting a goal. If we set our goal correctly, it will accelerate the speed at which our intuition will navigate towards it.

However, our fear is sending its own powerful signal as well. The good news is that we can learn to minimize or even disable that signal. Guided Intuition can give us complete control of our lives and allows us to achieve any goal we can imagine. This book will explain how.

Who is this book for?

If you feel stuck or suffer from low self-esteem or don't know what you want anymore, this book is for you.

The regular chaos of life can be overwhelming for some of us. Control is not always possible. The options facing us everyday can be maddening. It can cause enormous stress and frustration. For some, it can lead to depression and even suicidal thoughts when it all gets too much.

  • What if there was a way to define your goal more clearly?
  • What if it were possible to determine the shortest possible path towards that goal?
  • What if you could always know the right choices to make along that path?

This book will teach you how to define your goals and navigate successfully towards them without stress or fear. It will show you how to embrace change instead of avoiding it. Most importantly, you will learn how to trust yourself deeply.

The book teaches

how to let go of fear

how to stay calm in the midst of chaos

how to accept change

People with low self-esteem struggle to believe in themselves. They feel that they are never good enough or suffer from imposter syndrome. They are afraid of failure or making mistakes. They avoid risk and are afraid of change. They cling to the few things they feel that they can control. They have dreams of being much more but are afraid to pursue them. The fact is that any goal can be reached, if you know the right choices that will reliably lead you there but knowing the right choices feels like an overwhelming challenge. However, we live in a multi-dimensional reality of infinite probabilities. There are many timelines we might follow, forming an impossibly complex maze. We cannot see that maze because our conscious mind is limited to only three dimensions. If we were able to see in four dimensions that still wouldn’t be enough. Or even five.

We would have to be able to see in 6 dimensions before the maze would be fully understandable. And there is a part of us that lives in 6 dimensions and can see that maze - it’s our intuition. Our intuition knows where a given choice will ultimately lead. However, it will navigate to the place we BELIEVE we will go, not the place we WISH we will go. If we train ourselves to harness our intuition and we focus on having conviction about our destination, all obstacles will fall away. The actual discipline is in agreeing to make the right choice, to commit to it. To leave behind the fear that stands in our way and trust that your path is the right one for you.
